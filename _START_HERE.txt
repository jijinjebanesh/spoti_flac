🎯 5-MINUTE OVERVIEW:

WHAT YOU'RE ADDING:
─────────────────

1. Music Player Notification
   • Shows track name, artist, album art
   • Play/pause/skip buttons work
   • Position updates in real-time

2. Background Playback
   • Audio continues when app minimized
   • Audio continues when app closed
   • Still controlled via notification

3. Auto-Resume
   • Track resumes at saved position on app restart
   • Respects pause/play state
   • Session expires after 24 hours

4. Mini Player (optional)
   • Compact player widget on main screen
   • Shows current track + progress

5. Future Queue Support
   • Skip next/previous
   • Playlist support
   (built on same framework)
