WHAT'S FIXED:
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

❌ BEFORE (BROKEN):
   • PremiumPlaybackController created its own AudioPlayer
   • AudioBackgroundHandler had a separate AudioPlayer
   • Two players = two disconnected audio streams
   • When app minimized, system tracked handler's player
   • But UI was using controller's player
   • Result: Audio stops, no notification, complete failure

✅ AFTER (WORKING):
   • Single AudioPlayer owned by AudioBackgroundHandler
   • PremiumPlaybackController references handler's player
   • One player = one consistent audio stream
   • All playback uses same instance
   • mediaItem synced BEFORE play (notification works)
   • Result: Background audio continues, notifications appear
